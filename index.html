Hi! Nice To Meet You.

My name is Olivia Misasi and I am currently a Software Engineering student at the University of Waterloo

I am interested in combining software with artistry, whether that be UX/UI design, graphic design or animation. As an extroverted person, I enjoy getting to know others and art allows people to express themselves in their truest form. In my case, software happens to be the gateway. Being in this position as a developer allows me to not only better understand the person behind the user I am servicing, but also allows said users to learn more about me through what I create. Over the next five years in university, I hope to further myself in these fields and devote my time to continuously create and innovate.
